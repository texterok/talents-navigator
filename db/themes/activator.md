# Activator

**Domain:** Influencing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Activator theme can make things happen by turning thoughts into action. They want to do things now rather than simply talk about them.

## Description
"When can we start?" This recurring question reflects the impatient drive toward action. The theme emphasizes that "only action is real. Only action can make things happen. Only action leads to performance." Activators believe that action is the best device for learning — through a cycle of decision, action, result observation, and learning.

How people with Activator describe themselves:
- "I am impatient with inactivity."
- "I need less discussion, more action."
- "I love initiation, instigation."
- "I hate waiting, wasting time."
- "I bring a catalytic sense of urgency."

## Working With
Their perceived pushiness might intimidate others. Help them hone their approach to getting things started. Providing clear explanations for why action is needed helps others understand the logic behind initiatives.
