# Futuristic

**Domain:** Strategic Thinking

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Futuristic theme are inspired by the future and what could be. They energize others with their visions of the future.

## Description
Futuristic individuals "love to peer over the horizon" — the future fascinates them. They visualize detailed pictures of what tomorrow might hold, maintaining an inspirational perspective. These individuals are described as dreamers who "see visions of what could be" and cherish those visions, using them for personal energization and to uplift others around them.

How people with Futuristic describe themselves:
- "I am fascinated with tomorrow."
- "I need opportunities to talk about the future I see."
- "I love the inspiration that comes from dreaming."
- "I hate contentment with the status quo."
- "I bring previews, predictions and forecasts."

## Working With
Give them time to think, write about and plan for the products, services and processes needed in the future. Create opportunities for sharing their perspectives in team communications and industry forums.
