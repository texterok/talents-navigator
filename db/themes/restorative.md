# Restorative

**Domain:** Executing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Restorative theme are adept at dealing with problems. They are good at figuring out what is wrong and resolving it.

## Description
Individuals with this strength "love to solve problems" and can be energized by breakdowns rather than dismayed. They enjoy analyzing symptoms, identifying root causes, and implementing solutions. They experience fulfillment in restoring broken things — machines, techniques, people, or organizations — "back to life" and recognize their intervention prevents complete failure.

How people with Restorative describe themselves:
- "I am not intimidated by points of pain or dysfunction."
- "I need problems that must be solved."
- "I love finding solutions."
- "I hate the idea that problems will disappear if they are ignored."
- "I bring courage and creativity to problematic situations."

## Working With
Recognize that strong Restorative individuals may become overly self-critical. Coaching should redirect this inclination toward fixable knowledge/skill gaps or external, tangible problems rather than internal rumination.
