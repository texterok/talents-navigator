# Discipline

**Domain:** Executing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Discipline theme enjoy routine and structure. Their world is best described by the order they create.

## Description
Those with Discipline need predictability and order. They naturally impose structure through routines, timelines, and deadlines. They break long-term projects into specific short-term plans and work through each diligently. While not necessarily neat, they require precision and control to manage life's inherent messiness.

How people with Discipline describe themselves:
- "I am a meticulous organizer."
- "I need a structured and organized environment."
- "I love things that are organized and orderly."
- "I hate chaos, confusion and improvising."
- "I bring precision and detail orientation."

## Working With
Help clarify role expectations in detail. These individuals need a clear understanding of their responsibilities, how work fits together, and how expectations shift when circumstances change.

## At Their Best
Discipline talents thrive when using timelines to motivate and outlining step-by-step plans that keep teams focused. They excel at identifying inefficiencies and creating systems to improve productivity.
