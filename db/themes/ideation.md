# Ideation

**Domain:** Strategic Thinking

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Ideation theme are fascinated by ideas. They are able to find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena.

## Description
Individuals with strong Ideation are "delighted when you discover, beneath the complex surface, an elegantly simple concept to explain why things are the way they are." They excel at finding obscure connections between unrelated phenomena and enjoy "taking the world we all know and turning it around so we can view it from a strange but strangely enlightening angle."

How people with Ideation describe themselves:
- "I am unaffected by the ambiguity and risk of innovation."
- "I need freedom to explore possibilities without restraints or limits."
- "I love coming up with something brand new."
- "I hate doing what has always been done."
- "I bring new and fresh perspectives."

## Working With
Because they thrive on ideas, feed them new concepts that align with their goals and desired performance outcomes. This approach inspires them to generate new insights and discoveries independently.
