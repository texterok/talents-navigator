# Relator

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Relator theme enjoy close relationships with others. They find deep satisfaction in working hard with friends to achieve a goal.

## Description
"Relator describes your attitude toward your relationships. In simple terms, the Relator theme pulls you toward people you already know."

The theme emphasizes comfort with intimacy and willingness to deepen connections. It highlights that genuine relationships require risk and mutual understanding of feelings, goals, fears, and dreams.

"You probably prefer to be seen as a person, an equal or a friend rather than as a function."

How people with Relator describe themselves:
- "I need time and opportunities for one-on-one interactions."
- "I love close, caring, mutual relationships."
- "I hate the initial social discomfort of meeting someone new."

## Working With
Communicate genuine care for employees with strong Relator talents. These individuals organize their lives around close relationships, so clarity about their standing matters significantly.

## At Their Best
Relators bring "social depth and transparency" and act as catalysts for trusting relationships through genuine connection and proactive relationship-building efforts.
