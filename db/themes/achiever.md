# Achiever

**Domain:** Executing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Achiever theme work hard and possess a great deal of stamina. They take immense satisfaction in being busy and productive.

## Description
Your Achiever theme reflects an internal drive for constant accomplishment. Each day starts at zero, and you require tangible achievement by day's end to feel satisfied — this applies universally across workdays, weekends, and vacations. An "internal fire" pushes you toward continual achievement. After completing one goal, the motivation quickly rekindles for the next. This relentless need may lack logic or focus but remains persistent.

How people with Achiever describe themselves:
- "I am a hard worker."
- "I need freedom to work at my own pace."
- "I love completing tasks."
- "I hate a lack of diligence."
- "I bring the intensity and stamina of effort."

## Working With
When those with strong Achiever talents finish assignments, they rarely desire rest. Motivate them by recognizing past achievements and setting stretch goals that challenge them further.

## At Their Best
Achievers thrive when they have a steady stream of challenges and can see measurable progress. Setting progressively demanding goals after project completion keeps them engaged and productive.
