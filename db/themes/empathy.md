# Empathy

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Empathy theme can sense other people's feelings by imagining themselves in others' lives or situations.

## Description
You possess the ability to perceive emotions around you and experience them as if they were your own. Through intuitive perspective-taking, you can view situations through others' eyes and understand their viewpoints. However, this doesn't require agreement with their choices or feelings of pity. Your instinctive comprehension proves remarkably valuable.

You detect unspoken concerns, anticipate needs, and find appropriate language and tone where others struggle. You enable people to articulate their emotional experiences to themselves and others, making you naturally attractive to those around you.

How people with Empathy describe themselves:
- "I am an emotional person."
- "I need freedom to laugh, cry and vent."
- "I love the gladness, sadness and madness of humanity."
- "I hate things that block or limit emotional expression."
- "I bring emotional intelligence."

## Working With
Help these individuals recognize that their emotional sensing ability is a strength, not an embarrassment. Their talents provide colleagues with comfort and stability — show how this natural gift creates team value.
