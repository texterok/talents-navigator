# Positivity

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Positivity theme have contagious enthusiasm. They are upbeat and can get others excited about what they are going to do.

## Description
Individuals with strong Positivity are "generous with praise, quick to smile and always on the lookout for the positive in the situation." They inject enthusiasm into projects, celebrate achievements, and help lighten others' spirits. Their conviction that "it is good to be alive, that work can be fun" sustains them through setbacks without losing humor.

How people with Positivity describe themselves:
- "I am cheerful, hopeful and fun-loving."
- "I need freedom to experience the joy and drama of life."
- "I bring contagious energy and enthusiasm."

## Working With
Don't expect these individuals to enjoy "cheering up negative people." They perform better energizing those needing a spark. When their enthusiasm wanes, connect them with people sharing similar humor and positive outlooks.

## At Their Best
Positivity talents thrive when planning highlight activities, turning small achievements into celebrations, and providing motivation when others become discouraged or hesitant about risks.
