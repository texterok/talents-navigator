# Connectedness

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Connectedness theme have faith in the links among all things. They believe there are few coincidences and that almost every event has meaning.

## Description
"Things happen for a reason. You are sure of it... you know that we are all connected." While individuals possess free will, they are "part of something larger." People with this talent experience a sense of unity with humanity and understand that their role creates responsibilities — avoiding harm to others because "we will be harming ourselves."

These individuals are described as "considerate, caring, and accepting" bridge-builders across cultures who provide comfort about life's deeper purpose. Their faith "sustains you and your close friends in the face of life's mysteries."

How people with Connectedness describe themselves:
- Awareness of "the borderless and timeless human family"
- Need to belong "to something bigger than myself"
- Appreciation for "circles of life and threads of continuity"
- Rejection of divisive "us vs. them" thinking

## Working With
Encourage them to build bridges to different groups in the organization. They naturally think about how things connect, so they should excel at showing different people how each relies on the others. Help colleagues understand how their efforts fit in the larger picture.
