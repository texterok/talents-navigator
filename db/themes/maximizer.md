# Maximizer

**Domain:** Influencing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Maximizer theme focus on strengths as a way to stimulate personal and group excellence. They seek to transform something strong into something superb.

## Description
"Excellence, not average, is your measure." Transforming strong performance into superb performance is more rewarding than incremental improvements from a weak baseline.

"Strengths, whether yours or someone else's, fascinate you." People with this theme actively search for signs of strength and feel compelled to nurture and refine them toward excellence.

This talent means "others see you as discriminating." Maximizers choose to invest time with people who have cultivated their own strengths rather than those focused on fixing weaknesses.

How people with Maximizer describe themselves:
- "I am committed to excellence."
- "I need quality to be valued as much as quantity."
- "I love a maximum return on investments."
- "I hate an obsession with fixing weakness."

## Working With
When engaging Maximizers in performance conversations, remember they are "interested in taking something that works and figuring out ways to maximize its performance. They might not be interested in fixing broken things."
