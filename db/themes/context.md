# Context

**Domain:** Strategic Thinking

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Context theme enjoy thinking about the past. They understand the present by researching its history.

## Description
Those with Context look backward to find answers. They seek "blueprints" and initial intentions from earlier times to understand present confusion. This historical perspective brings confidence and better decision-making by revealing underlying structure. Context develops wiser judgment about the future through observing "seeds" sown in the past.

How people with Context describe themselves:
- "I am appreciative of my predecessors and prior events."
- "I need relevant background for discussions and decisions."
- "I love the retrospective."
- "I hate when the past is forgotten."
- "I bring accurate memories and valuable memorabilia."

## Working With
When problems arise, ask Context-talented employees to research contributing factors and prior actions. This historical analysis equips teams to make improved future choices based on learned lessons.

## At Their Best
Context individuals thrive when teaching through case studies, using past behavior to predict future outcomes, and channeling their historical perspective into positive forward change rather than dwelling in history.
