# Self-Assurance

**Domain:** Influencing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Self-Assurance theme feel confident in their ability to take risks and manage their own lives. They have an inner compass that gives them certainty in their decisions.

## Description
The theme encompasses confidence in both abilities and judgment. Those with Self-Assurance possess a unique perspective and understand they alone hold authority over their decisions. This creates an unwavering sense of certainty that isn't easily swayed by external arguments. The theme is described as "like the keel of a ship," providing stability amid various pressures.

How people with Self-Assurance describe themselves:
- "I am confident on the inside when there is doubt all around me."
- "I need freedom to act unilaterally and independently."
- "I love being in control of my own destiny."

## Working With
Help them find tasks or projects where they can make decisions and control the work. They excel in start-up situations without established rules or procedures, leveraging their natural independence and decisive capabilities.

## At Their Best
Self-Assurance individuals thrive when given opportunities for independent thinking and action, particularly in situations requiring confidence and self-direction without requiring external oversight or validation.
