# Individualization

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Individualization theme are intrigued with the unique qualities of each person. They have a gift for figuring out how different people can work together productively.

## Description
Your talent "leads you to be intrigued by the unique qualities of each person. You are impatient with generalizations or 'types'" and instead focus on differences between individuals. You naturally "observe each person's style, each person's motivation, how each thinks, and how each builds relationships."

Because of your observation skills, "you can draw out the best in each person" and know that "the secret to great teams is casting by individual strengths so that everyone can do a lot of what they do well."

How people with Individualization describe themselves:
- "I am a customizer."
- "I hate a one-size-fits-all approach."
- "I bring an understanding of people and where they fit best."

## Working With
When facing performance issues with individual employees, turn to Individualization-talented team members for insight — "they can show you the world through the employee's eyes and help you consider the appropriate action for each individual."
