# Developer

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Developer theme recognize and cultivate the potential in others. They spot the signs of each small improvement and derive satisfaction from evidence of progress.

## Description
Developers see individuals as works in progress. They help others succeed by creating stretching experiences and monitoring growth signs — new skills, performance improvements, or moments of excellence. These incremental advances fuel their satisfaction and strength.

How people with Developer describe themselves:
- "I am patient with the inexperienced and unseasoned."
- "I need someone to invest in."
- "I love to see potential and progress in people."
- "I hate wasted or unrealized potential."
- "I bring a commitment (time and energy) to the growth of others."

## Working With
Encourage them to notice colleague improvements and provide specific recognition. Coach them to celebrate others' achievements through their natural observation skills.

Avoid supporting struggling individuals indefinitely without guidance — help them direct investment toward better-fitting roles to prevent blind spots from wasted investment.
