# Woo

**Domain:** Influencing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Woo theme love the challenge of meeting new people and winning them over. They derive satisfaction from breaking the ice and making a connection with someone.

## Description
Woo stands for "winning others over." Individuals with this strength "enjoy the challenge of meeting new people and getting them to like you. Strangers are rarely intimidating to you." Rather, "strangers can be energizing" and people with Woo "are drawn to them."

Those with strong Woo talents "derive satisfaction from breaking the ice and making a connection." They are "rarely at a loss for words" and "actually enjoy initiating with strangers." Once connections form, they're comfortable moving forward to "meet new people" and experience "new crowds to mingle in."

How people with Woo describe themselves:
- "I am an engaging greeter."
- "I need new people to meet, new rooms to work, new crowds to mingle in."
- "I love meeting someone I haven't met before."
- "I hate a static or shrinking social network."
- "I bring energy to social situations."

## Working With
Challenge employees with strong Woo talents to build their professional network and help them find ways to meet new people and make new connections each day. This will energize them.
