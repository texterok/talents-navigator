# Analytical

**Domain:** Strategic Thinking

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Analytical theme search for reasons and causes. They have the ability to think about all of the factors that might affect a situation.

## Description
"Your Analytical theme challenges other people: 'Prove it. Show me why what you are claiming is true.'" People with this talent view themselves as objective and dispassionate, preferring data because "they are value free. They have no agenda."

These individuals "search for patterns and connections" and want to understand how patterns affect one another and their outcomes. They "peel the layers back until, gradually, the root cause or causes are revealed."

Others perceive them as "logical and rigorous" and seek them out "to expose someone's 'wishful thinking' or 'clumsy thinking' to your refining mind."

How people with Analytical describe themselves:
- "I am logical and objective in my approach."
- "I need time to think."
- "I love data and facts."
- "I hate things that are not or cannot be proven."
- "I bring dispassionate thinking to emotional issues."

## Working With
Because the accuracy of work is so important to them, they may prioritize getting a task done correctly over meeting a deadline. Partner them with those having strong Activator talents to ensure their analysis doesn't turn into paralysis.
