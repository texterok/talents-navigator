# Command

**Domain:** Influencing

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Command theme have presence. They can take control of a situation and make decisions.

## Description
"Command leads you to take charge. Unlike some people, you feel no discomfort with imposing your views on others. Once your opinion is formed, you need to share it with others."

"You are not frightened by confrontation; rather, you know that confrontation is the first step toward resolution."

"You have presence. You have Command."

How people with Command describe themselves:
- "I am direct and decisive."
- "I need challenges and conflicts."
- "I love exerting control in situations that seem out of control."
- "I hate passivity and avoidance."
- "I bring emotional clarity."

## Working With
Encourage people with dominant Command talents to take on important challenges and bring others along with them. They have talents that can lead others out of their comfort zone and into new territory. Pairing them with colleagues possessing Woo or Empathy talents can help them navigate obstacles through relationships rather than confrontation alone.

## At Their Best
Command talent shines when addressing uncomfortable truths, leading committees, spearheading new initiatives, and in situations demanding emotional clarity and strong direction-setting.
