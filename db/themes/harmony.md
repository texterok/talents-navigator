# Harmony

**Domain:** Relationship Building

## Definition
People exceptionally talented in the Harmony theme look for consensus. They don't enjoy conflict; rather, they seek areas of agreement.

## Description
People with Harmony talent view conflict as wasteful and unproductive. They actively seek common ground among differing viewpoints and work to steer conversations away from confrontation. These individuals prioritize consensus-building and believe groups function more effectively when members align around shared values rather than debate opposing positions. They willingly adjust personal objectives to support group harmony, though only when core values align.

How people with Harmony describe themselves:
- "I am calm and even-keeled."
- "I need areas of agreement and common ground."
- "I love the sacrifice of personal agendas to facilitate group performance."

## Working With
When conflicts arise, encourage employees with Harmony to leverage their natural ability to identify common ground among disagreeing parties. They can help teams find areas of agreement that serve as starting points for renewed collaboration, though they may not resolve the underlying debate itself.

## At Their Best
Harmony talents thrive when widening conversations to include more voices, creating forums where opinions feel genuinely heard, and developing conflict-resolution techniques that prevent passive-aggressive responses.
